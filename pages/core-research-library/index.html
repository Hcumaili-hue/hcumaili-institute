CORE RESEARCH LIBRARY

Core Research Programs

Major institutional studies on divine revelation, theology, prophetic continuity, semantics, law, divine mediation, and philosophy of religion.

01

God First, Then Religion

A comparative Abrahamic study of revelation, scripture, prophetic continuity, sacred law, and theological development.

02

The Definition of God

A comparative investigation into the Abrahamic conception of God across revelation, scripture, and theology.

03

The Divine Names

A study of divine naming, sacred language, attributes, and theological meaning.

04

Gabriel and Divine Mediation

A study of revelation, angelic mediation, divine communication, and prophetic transmission.

05

Expected Prophet

A comparative investigation into messianic and prophetic expectation across scripture.

06

Prophetic Continuity

The continuity of revelation and prophetic tradition across Abrahamic history.

07

Prophetic Science

A theoretical study of prophecy, revelation, sacred authority, and transmission.

08

Comparative Law

Comparative study of divine law, covenant, ethics, and legal continuity.

09

Human Interpretation

Human reception, interpretation, theological development, and scripture.

10

Prophetic History

Historical development of revelation and prophetic movements across civilizations.
